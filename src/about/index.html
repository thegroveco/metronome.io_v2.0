About Metronome.

The self-governing, reliable, and portable cryptocurrency.

Leading figures in the cryptocurrency space built Metronome. A diverse array of partners and advisors support Metronome because it was engineered to meet and exceed the high standards of the cryptocurrency community.

In developing an enduring cryptocurrency, three core design principles build Metronome’s foundation:

Self-Governance

With no undue influence from founders after initial launch, Metronome’s founders transitioned to community members, and each MET owner chooses for themselves where they hold their MET.

Reliability

With a highly predictability issuance and supply, community members, individuals, and groups can calculate the supply of MET at any date in the future with a high degree of confidence.

Portability

Metronome enables maximum decentralization, even across different blockchains.

Metronome is the first cryptocurrency capable of being exported and imported across chains.

Metronome was initially issued on Ethereum with Ethereum Classic, Rootstock on Bitcoin, and Qtum support expected to follow. Users can select the chain that suits their requirements for management and security, or even upgrade the MET contract if needed. Metronome’s main goal is to be a built-to-last cryptocurrency. Portability furthers this goal by freeing Metronome from the fate of any one chain.
